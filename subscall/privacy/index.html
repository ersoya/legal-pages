Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Subscall ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Subscription data you enter into Subscall (such as subscriptions, prices, renewal dates, and reminder preferences) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We may also process limited technical and usage information through third-party services to operate and improve the app, as described in the Third-Party Services section below.

2. In-App Purchases

Subscall offers auto-renewable subscriptions. Payments will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscriptions at any time in your App Store account settings.

We do not have access to your payment information.

3. Data Usage

We use limited usage and diagnostic data to understand how the app is used, improve performance, and fix issues. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only with the third-party service providers listed in this policy, and only to the extent necessary to provide their functionality.

5. Data Security

All subscription data is stored securely on your device using Apple's secure storage systems. We do not have access to your data.

6. Third-Party Services

Subscall uses trusted third-party services to improve the app experience.

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand app usage.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix crashes.
  • RevenueCat – to manage subscriptions and purchases.

These services only process the information necessary to provide their functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Subscall is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on this page.
