Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fately ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Fately does not require user accounts. Most information you create in the app — including your display name, fate reveal history, streak data, and reminder preferences — is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We may process limited technical and diagnostic information through third-party services to operate and improve the app, as described in the Third-Party Services section below.

2. Local Data

The following data is stored on your device using Apple's secure local storage:

  • Display name (optional)
  • Fate reveal history and daily category selections
  • Streak and engagement data
  • Notification and reminder preferences

We do not upload or sync this data to our servers.

3. Notifications

Fately may send local notifications to remind you about daily fate reveals, if you enable this feature. Notifications are scheduled on your device and can be disabled at any time through the app or your device settings.

4. Data Usage

We use limited usage and diagnostic data to understand how the app is used, improve performance, and fix issues. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only with the third-party service providers listed in this policy, and only to the extent necessary to provide their functionality.

6. Data Security

Your app data is stored securely on your device. We do not have access to your fate history, display name, or other personal content stored locally in the app.

7. Third-Party Services

Fately uses trusted third-party services to improve the app experience.

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand app usage.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix crashes.

These services only process the information necessary to provide their functionality. Fate content and your display name are not included in analytics events.

8. Children's Privacy

Fately is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on this page.
