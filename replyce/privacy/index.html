Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Replyce ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Replyce does not require user accounts and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name or email.

However, limited data may be processed:

  • Anonymous usage data
  • Crash logs
  • Text inputs for AI response generation

2. AI Processing

When using Replyce, your input may be sent to third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) to generate responses. These requests are not linked to your identity.

3. In-App Purchases

Replyce offers auto-renewable subscriptions. Payments are handled by Apple.

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled
  • You can manage subscriptions in App Store settings

We do not have access to your payment information.

4. Data Usage

We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with the third-party service providers listed in this policy, and only to the extent necessary to provide their functionality.

6. Data Security

Your data is processed securely using industry-standard technologies.

7. Third-Party Services

Replyce uses trusted third-party services to improve the app experience.

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand app usage.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix crashes.
  • RevenueCat – to manage subscriptions and purchases.
  • OpenAI – to generate AI response suggestions from your text input.

These services only process the information necessary to provide their functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Replyce is not intended for children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
